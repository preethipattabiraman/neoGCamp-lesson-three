A 100 word Story

She looked absolutely beautiful in the green party dress that hugged her curves intimately. Her emerald earrings matched her outfit perfectly. The slit in her dress was enticing.

She was the centre of attraction.

Just then, a man came and asked for her hand in the dance. She looked up, "Why me?"

"You're really beautiful. Rest are shapeless after becoming mothers. After all, motherhood is the exit card for sexiness. You're a student? Working somewhere?", he asked.

"Stay-at-home-mom of two", she replied proudly reaching for her kids.

"You forgot this," her husband reminded as he filled her Maang with love.
